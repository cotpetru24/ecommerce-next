Declaration

You have a duty to give a fair presentation of the risk to an insurer based on your reasonable understanding of the risks to your business...

A material circumstance is defined as:

  • Special or unusual circumstances relating to the risk
  • Any particular concerns which led you to seek insurance cover for the risk
  • Anything which those concerned with this insurance would generally understand as material

If you fail to disclose information or misrepresent any fact, this could invalidate the policy and mean that claims may not be paid in full.
